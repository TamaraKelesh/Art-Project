Art, also called (to distinguish it from other art forms) visual art, a visual object or experience consiously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms arePainting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theater, Film and Music. However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the liberal Arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic Rhetoric, Arithmetic, Geometry, Astronomy and Music.
